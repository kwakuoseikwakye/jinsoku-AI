## The Problem

Global teams waste so many hours every year because of language barriers. Most tools out there make you choose between juggling a separate translation app during your video call or relying on basic captions that miss half the meaning.

Jinsoku fixes that. Translation, transcription, and voice cloning all live inside the meeting itself. It's not something bolted on after the fact.

---

## Features

### Real-time Voice Translation
Just speak naturally. Jinsoku translates what you say into 42 languages in under 300 milliseconds. Everyone in the meeting hears the audio in their own language and sees live captions as the conversation flows.

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/image1.png" alt="Live Translate - Japanese to English in real time" width="800" />
</p>

### AI Voice Cloning
Upload a 1-minute audio sample of your voice and Jinsoku builds a digital version of it. Other people hear *you* speaking, with your tone, rhythm, and personality intact, not some robotic voice. That works across every supported language.

### Live Captions & Translation
Subtitles show up in real time, already translated into each person's language. When the meeting wraps up, you get a full transcript too.

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/image4.png" alt="Live captions - translated conversation between Sarah and Hiroshi" width="800" />
</p>

### HD Video & Audio Conferencing
Clear video and audio that adapts to your connection automatically. You can have up to 100 people in a single meeting with solid, low-latency performance no matter where they are.

### AI Meeting Summaries
No more note-taking. After each meeting, Jinsoku pulls together a summary with the key points, action items, and decisions. It's all done automatically.

### Video Messages
Record a video and send it to anyone. Jinsoku translates it into each recipient's language using your cloned voice. Perfect when your team is spread across time zones.

### Meeting Recordings
Every recording comes with captions and translations baked in, so you can go back and review any moment in any language.

## FAQ

**How accurate is the translation?**
We use neural models built specifically for real conversation. For popular language pairs like English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese, the output is close to what you'd get from a professional human translator in a business setting.

**Does the voice cloning actually sound real?**
It does. A 1-minute sample is all it takes for Jinsoku to learn your tone, pitch, and speaking style. It carries that across all 42 languages.

**How fast is the translation?**
Under 300 milliseconds. You speak, and the other person hears it almost immediately in their language. There's barely any gap.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. Everything is end-to-end encrypted and we're SOC 2 and GDPR compliant. We don't sell your data, we don't use it for training, and you can delete it whenever you want.

**Can I try it without paying?**
Absolutely. The Free plan gives you 30 minutes of translation and 5 hours of meetings each month. No credit card needed. If you go with a paid plan, there's a 14-day money-back guarantee.

**Does it work for big meetings?**
The Bolt plan handles up to 100 participants with no time limit. If you need something even bigger, reach out and we'll figure it out.
